About Us

Feediot is an e-commerce marketplace where the products are hosted along with the description as seen on different e-commerce websites. The uniqueness of Feediot is in every product. Some of the products are made exclusively from virgin goods; others are entirely hand-made; some of the products can be reused for years on end without considerable wear.

Being locally produced, every product has a tailored finish to it and are relatively cheaper than their mass-produced alternatives.
